API Usage Guide:

YouTube:

Get video data: /yt/v-get?id={videoId}&parts=snippet,statistics

TikTok:

Get video info: /tt/v-get?url={videoUrl}

Download video (no watermark): /tt/v-download?url={videoUrl}

Download video (HD): /tt/v-download?url={videoUrl}&quality=hd

Example:

  • YouTube: /yt/v-get?id=abc123&parts=snippet,statistics
  • TikTok (Info): /tt/v-get?url=https://www.tiktok.com/@user/video/123456
  • TikTok (Download): /tt/v-download?url=https://www.tiktok.com/@user/video/123456
  • TikTok (Download HD): /tt/v-download?url=https://www.tiktok.com/@user/video/123456&quality=hd
